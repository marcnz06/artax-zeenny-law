User Management
1. How do I log in to the ARTAX system?

To log in to the ARTAX system, visit our login page and enter your username and password. If you encounter any login issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

2. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password. If you need further assistance, reach out to our support team.

3. How do I access and update my profile information in ARTAX?

You can access and update your profile information by logging in and going to your user profile. Here, you can edit your personal details, preferences, and, later on, other relevant information.
